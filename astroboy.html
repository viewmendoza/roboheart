Kid Atom

Tetsuwan Atom, is a Japanese manga series first published in 1952. The story follows the adventures of a robot named Atom. Tetsuwan Atom was adapted into the first popular animated Japanese television series that embodied the aesthetic that later became familiar worldwide as anime. It was created by Osamu Tezuka, referred to as the "God of Manga" in Japan.After enjoying success abroad, Tetsuwan Atom was remade in the 1980s as Shin Tetsuwan Atomu, known as Astroboy in other countries, and again in 2003 under the title "Astro Boy-Tetsuwan Atomu".
